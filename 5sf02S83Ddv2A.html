Band Profile

About WIND BREAKER

WIND BREAKER is a southern metal band formed in Nottingham, England. Known for their explosive stage energy and sarcastic lyrics about Christianity, they combine heavy guitar riffs with haunting melodies to create a unique and powerful sound.

Inspiration & Background

Inspired by metal giants such as As I Lay Dying and Killswitch Engage, as well as the melodic touches of post-hardcore bands, WIND BREAKER tells stories of internal struggles, resilience, and hope. Their music is cathartic, both for the band members and their listeners.

Author & Lyricist

All songs featured here were composed and written entirely by Adam Zibran. With a deep passion for musical composition and lyric writing, each piece is a reflection of his personal artistic vision, brought to life through collaboration with the musicians of WIND BREAKER.
